Heart disease kills more women than
any other disease, and yet women are
chronically misdiagnosed.



We’re on a mission to reduce preventable heart disease in women.

Share your heart health experience to empower women to take
charge of their medical journeys.

















Contrary to common belief, women and men are equally affected by heart
disease regardless of geographic location or economic status.

Survivors Stories



Surviving a biased healthcare system is not guaranteed.


ModernCardio aims to increase rates of survival by using machine learning technology for early detection and preventative care.














Be Part of the Modern Solution


In order to build equitable and inclusive preventative health solutions, we need people of all kinds to share their heart health experiences. We’re accepting contributions of all kinds: personal testimonials, physicial notes, bloodwork results, imaging, or anything else that illustrates a cardio health journey.



Read the ModernCardio submission guide

This submission guide provides suggestions on how to anonymize your story and health data before submitting it to ModernCardio. Data privacy is a top concern for all.



Gather your documentation materials

What do you want to share and why do you believe it would be helpful in reducing misdiagnosis? We’re accepting anything you’re willing to provide.



Submit documentation of your experience

Use our secure form to upload your anonymized files and contribute to our growing database of female cardio patient experiences.



Invite others to participate

Help us build our knowledge base by posting about your contribution online. Don’t forget to tag your family and friends!
